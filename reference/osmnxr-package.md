# osmnxr: Download, Model and Analyze 'OpenStreetMap' Street Networks

A 'tidyverse'-friendly toolkit, inspired by the 'OSMnx' 'Python'
library, to download, model, simplify, analyze and visualize street
networks and other geospatial features from 'OpenStreetMap'. Build
routable graphs from a place name, address, point or bounding box;
simplify topology; compute shortest paths, isochrones and urban metrics
(intersection density, circuity, street-orientation entropy,
centrality); and export to 'sf', 'sfnetworks' and 'MapLibre'. Heavy
graph computation is performed by a bundled 'Rust' core.
